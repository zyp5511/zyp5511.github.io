Yipeng Zhang

I am currently a Senior AI Engineer at Zoom, working on agentic workflows, LLM-based user simulation, and large-scale evaluation of automated systems. Previously, I was an Applied Researcher 2 at eBay Search Science, leading end-to-end development of multimodal product understanding and agentic search.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of California, Los Angeles under the supervision of Prof. Vwani Roychowdhury. Before that, I earned my M.S. from University of California, San Diego and my B.S. from University of Wisconsin-Madison.

I drive end-to-end ownership of production AI systems, from problem formulation and model development to deployment, evaluation, and continuous iteration at scale. My work focuses on building reliable LLM and multimodal pipelines with rigorous evaluation and measurable product impact in cross-functional environments. In parallel, I develop interpretable EEG/iEEG models for epilepsy in clinical settings.

Across my research and applied work, I have authored 10+ first/co-first author publications and filed multiple U.S. patents.
